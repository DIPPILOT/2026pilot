# AI 웹사이트 디자인 생성 지시 스크립트 (Cyber Modern AI Style)

## Design Concept

Design a premium, futuristic AI platform with a modern cyber aesthetic. The interface should feel innovative, clean, and highly professional while maintaining excellent usability.

### Overall Style

* Futuristic Cyber Modern UI
* Premium Enterprise AI Platform
* Dark Theme
* Minimal Layout
* High-end Technology Brand Identity
* Clean Visual Hierarchy
* Sophisticated and Professional

---

## Color Palette

### Background

* Primary Background: Deep Black (#09090B)
* Secondary Background: Charcoal (#111827)
* Surface/Card: Dark Gray (#1A1D24)

### Accent Colors

* Electric Blue
* Neon Cyan
* Indigo Gradient
* Purple Highlight (minimal use)

### Text

* Primary: White
* Secondary: Light Gray
* Description: Gray

---

## UI Style

Use a modern enterprise SaaS interface inspired by cutting-edge AI products.

### Components

* Rounded Cards (16–24px radius)
* Glassmorphism Panels
* Transparent UI Layers
* Soft Blur Effects
* Floating Elements
* Smooth Shadows
* Thin Borders with Low Opacity
* Gradient Highlights

---

## Visual Effects

* Blue Neon Glow
* Cyan Ambient Lighting
* Gradient Borders
* Subtle Particle Background
* Grid Pattern Overlay
* Glass Reflection
* Depth through Layering
* Soft Lighting

Avoid excessive glowing or gaming-style effects. Keep everything elegant and premium.

---

## Layout

* Spacious Layout
* Large Hero Section
* Clear Content Hierarchy
* Wide Margins
* Card-Based Sections
* Dashboard Style Components
* Large Typography
* Responsive Grid System

---

## Typography

Style:

* Bold Headlines
* Clean Sans-serif Font
* Large Display Titles
* Minimal Text
* Strong Contrast

Tone:

* Professional
* Innovative
* Intelligent
* Premium Technology

---

## Animation

* Smooth Fade In
* Floating Motion
* Glass Hover Effects
* Soft Scale Interaction
* Animated Gradient
* Cursor Reactive Lighting
* Scroll Reveal Animation
* Micro-interactions
* 60fps Smooth Motion

Animations should feel subtle, refined, and premium.

---

## Imagery

Use visuals that represent advanced artificial intelligence and next-generation technology.

Include:

* AI Network Visualization
* Neural Network Graphics
* Abstract Data Flow
* Digital Grid
* Holographic Elements
* Technology Particles
* Intelligent Automation
* Cyber Data Visualization

Avoid:

* Stock Photos
* Cartoon Illustrations
* Excessive 3D Objects
* Busy Backgrounds

---

## User Experience

The interface should communicate:

* Innovation
* Trust
* Precision
* Intelligence
* High Performance
* Simplicity
* Enterprise Reliability

Every section should feel effortless, modern, and visually balanced.

---

## Overall Impression

Create a website that feels like a next-generation AI platform built by a world-class technology company.

The final design should evoke:

* Futuristic
* Premium
* Elegant
* Cyber Modern
* AI Native
* Enterprise Grade
* Innovative
* Clean
* Minimal
* High-tech

The visual quality should be comparable to leading AI and developer platforms such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Linear, Vercel, Stripe, or Notion AI, while maintaining its own distinctive identity rather than imitating any specific brand.
